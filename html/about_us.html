SigmaCR is being developed by us as our final year research and development project. There are number of objectives which we want to meet

  • Research on the applicability of Ontologies to identify features of a product.
  • Research on the applicability of both Machine Learning techniques and general rule based techniques to extract product features and the customers' opinions and the sentiment on them from reviews.
  • Research on analysing customer sentiments on the extracted features.
  • Identify different implementation methods and Engineering processors by doing a literature review and come up with a best solution.
  • Develop an extensible architecture for the project so that the system can be extended for any kind of product and multiple review corpus collection methodologies can be incorporated.
  • Automate or semi automate the system, so that it would adopt to different products.
